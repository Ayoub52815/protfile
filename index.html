About Me

Hello! I'm Ayoub Zouihri, also known as Sukuna. I'm a 17-year-old developer from Morocco currently in my final year of high school. Despite my young age, I've already developed several projects including WhatsApp bots, games, applications, and websites.

My passion is creating digital solutions that entertain and help people. I'm currently working on educational content to teach others what I've learned on my programming journey.

My main project is a school management system website, along with several entertainment WhatsApp bots. I'm also planning to launch a YouTube channel soon where I'll share tutorials and tech content.

What drives me is the desire to learn and share knowledge. I believe technology should be accessible to everyone, and I'm working to make that happen through my projects and future educational content.
